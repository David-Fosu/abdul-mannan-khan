About Me

I have extensive expertise in robotics development, skilled in Python, C++, and Verilog HDL, along with mechanical and electrical design. My experience includes designing robotic systems with FPGAs and microcontrollers (STM32, PIC18F452, Arduino, Raspberry Pi, Jetson Nano/AGX), and employing tools like SLAM and navigation stacks. I have successfully applied reinforcement learning algorithms on various platforms, notably the Soft Actor Critic (SAC) on mobile robots and Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) on legged robots. My work at Hanbat National University and the University of Bristol showcases these applications.

My diverse background spans industry and academia, holding key roles at LigNex1, HEXAR in South Korea, the National Development Complex in Pakistan, and in the UK, demonstrating a commitment to pushing the boundaries of robotics technology.
